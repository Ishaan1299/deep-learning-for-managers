**Suggested Projects in Indian Context with 2018–2024 Data** 

**1\. Credit Default Prediction in Indian Banks** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Rising NPAs since 2018 have pressured Indian banks. ▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Default probability models to guide lending and credit  policy. 

**2\. UPI Fraud Detection** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: UPI crossed 100 billion transactions (2018–2023); fraud is  rising. 

▪ **Dataset**: NPCI monthly UPI reports (2018–2024). https://www.npci.org.in/what we-do/upi/product-statistics 

▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Fintech fraud prevention policies. 

**3\. Employee Attrition in Indian IT Sector** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Attrition spiked post-COVID in IT firms.   
▪ **Dataset**: consider Glassdoor India reviews (2019–2024), Kaggle HR Attrition (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/pavansubhasht/ibm-hr-analytics-attrition dataset).   
▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Retention strategies for HR 

**4\. Skill Gap Analysis in India** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: AICTE/NASSCOM reports (2018–2023) show  employability \<50%. 

▪ **Dataset**: AICTE/NASSCOM surveys (if found), LinkedIn India job data (Via  API). 

▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Curriculum redesign, reskilling program  recommendations. 

**5\. E-commerce Customer Sentiment (Flipkart, Amazon India)** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Customer loyalty is critical in India’s e-commerce boom. ▪ **Dataset**: Amazon/Flipkart India reviews (2018–2024 Kaggle datasets). ▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Product improvement insights, targeted promotions,  recommendation systems. 

**6\. Food Delivery (Zomato/Swiggy)** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Reviews on Zomato/Swiggy affect retention. ▪ **Dataset**: Kaggle Zomato dataset (2019–2023) 

(https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shrutimehta/zomato-restaurants-data) , scraped  Swiggy reviews 

▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Consumer preference insights, service optimization. 

**7\. OTT Success Prediction (JioHotstar, Netflix India)** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: OTT competition surged post-2018.   
▪ **Dataset**: IMDb India (2018–2024), Twitter hashtags Academic API (\#NetflixIndia, \#Hotstar). 

▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: ROI forecasting for new shows. (using Regression,  deep learning NLP)  
**8\. Lifestyle Disease Analytics (India)** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Rise of diabetes/heart disease in urban India post-2018. ▪ **Dataset**: ICMR-NCDIR datasets (2018–2023). 

▪ **Techniques**: Logistic regression, DL-based classifiers.   
▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Insurance pricing and preventive care strategies. 

**9\. Tourism Demand Forecasting (Indian States)** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Tourism slumped in 2020, rebounded post-2022. ▪ **Dataset**: Ministry of Tourism reports (2018–2024), *IndiaStat*. 

▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Destination marketing plans, seasonal strategy. (Using  ARIMA, Prophet Analysis, LSTM) 

**10\. Hotel Review Sentiment (TripAdvisor India)** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Reviews shape consumer choice in Indian tourism. ▪ **Dataset**: TripAdvisor/Goibibo reviews (2018–2024) (do Scrapping); TripAdvisor  Hotel Reviews (Kaggle) (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/andrewmvd/trip advisor-hotel-reviews). 

▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Service improvement dashboards for hotels. 

**11\. Market Price Forecasting (Mandi Data)** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Price volatility impacts farmers’ income.   
▪ **Dataset**: Agmarknet.gov.in daily mandi prices (2018–2024)    
(https://agmarknet.gov.in/)   
▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Farmer advisory systems, supply chain planning. 

**12\. Crime Analytics in Indian States** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Crime patterns impact policy and safety.   
▪ **Dataset**: NCRB reports (2018–2023), *IndiaStat*.   
▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Predictive policing recommendations. 

**13\. Smart City Traffic Prediction** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Congestion costs India 1.5% GDP annually.   
▪ **Dataset**: Smart City project data (2019–2024), Google Maps API traffic data. ▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Infrastructure planning insights. (suggested techniques LSTMs, Graph Neural Networks) 

**14\. Renewable Energy Forecasting (India)** 

▪ **Problem Statement**: Solar/wind energy adoption accelerated post-2018. ▪ **Dataset**: MNRE (https://mnre.gov.in/), POSOCO (Power System Operation  Corporation) Grid data (2018–2024) (https://grid-india.in/hi/). 

▪ **Managerial Deliverables**: Energy policy, load balancing strategies.
